About the Band

We don't just play — we create a moment

Hydromiel is a live music collective rooted in the richest traditions of soul, funk, jazz, and groove. From the silky warmth of neo-soul to the irresistible pulse of disco, every set is a seamless journey — the kind of evening your guests will be talking about long after last call.

Whether we're setting the mood for an intimate wedding reception or turning a bar into a dance floor, we read the room, we feel the energy, and we bring the magic. Honey-smooth vocals, tight rhythms, and enough groove to keep the dance floor packed until the lights come on.
